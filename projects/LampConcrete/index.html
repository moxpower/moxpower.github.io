--- layout: page title: "Concrete Lamp" description: "Building with concrete is tricky. At a point of no return, wet concrete is poured into a cast. I chose to build a lamp frame with complicated dimensions. I had to think very well to know what the cast needs to look like and what wooden boards I need to build it. Once assembled and sealed with silicon I poured the concrete into the cast. The concrete dried after two days and I could remove the screws from the cast. I had to destroy the inner boards with a chisle as they were enclosed too tight to pull them out.
